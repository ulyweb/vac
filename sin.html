Singapore Customs Declaration Help — first-timer version

This is visible on the page so relatives do not have to hunt for it. It explains what to expect before they fill the country form or reach customs.

What Singapore is asking

SG Arrival Card handles arrival/health information, but customs/GST/cash questions are separate. If carrying over SGD 20,000 equivalent in physical currency or bearer negotiable instruments, submit the cash declaration. New goods, gifts, souvenirs, food, liquor, tobacco, and controlled goods may also need declaration/payment.

Open SG Arrival CardSingapore Customs arriving infoCash declaration info

Usually personal belongings

  • Used phone, iPad, laptop, camera, watch, chargers for your own trip.
  • Clothes, toiletries, shoes, and normal travel items.
  • Items you owned before arriving and will take out again.

Declare / ask customs

  • Cash over SGD 20,000 equivalent.
  • New expensive goods, gifts, souvenirs, boxed electronics, liquor/tobacco.
  • Food, animal/plant products, medicine that may be controlled, or anything for sale.

Important warning

  • Do not bring vapes/e-cigarettes.
  • No duty-free concession or GST relief for cigarettes/tobacco products.
  • Keep receipts for new purchases; if above relief, use Red Channel/Customs@SG if directed.
