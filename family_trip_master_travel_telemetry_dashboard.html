Eligibility

Passport, visa, and nationality checks

Do not let the dashboard guess citizenship-specific visa rules. Check every traveler individually.

+

Passport

Check expiration date and blank pages. Singapore explicitly lists minimum 6-month passport validity for non-Singapore passport holders.

Visa

Visa rules depend on nationality, passport type, and length/purpose of stay. Check Philippines, Japan, Singapore, and U.S. return requirements for every traveler.

Children / minors

Carry birth certificate copies and any consent/custody documents if children are traveling with one parent, relatives, or guardians. Requirements can vary by airline and border officer.

Official-site rule

Use only official government portals. Some third-party sites charge unnecessary fees for forms that are free through official channels.
